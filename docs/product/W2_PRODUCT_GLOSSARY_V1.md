# W2 Product Glossary V1

Market Probability: probability implied by market odds after devig.
Independent Model Probability: probability from W2 independent model, separate from market.
Calibrated Probability: model probability after calibration.
Final Probability: system-selected probability for display, with provenance.
Candidate: system-created possible direction.
Recommendation: one official primary direction after rules and AI review.
Watch: observed but not official.
Skip: evaluated and intentionally not recommended.
Reference Score: scenario from model catalog, not exact-score recommendation.
First Seen Odds: first collected odds in W2, not Opening Odds.
Opening Odds: verified market opening from provider/history.
Closing Odds: final pre-kickoff market snapshot.
As-of Time: time the prediction claims to know data.
Data Cutoff: latest allowed source time.
Feature Snapshot: immutable prematch feature bundle.
Model Run: versioned model execution.
Recommendation Lock: immutable prematch card lock.
Settlement: postmatch evaluation.
Evidence: system fact cited by AI.
Invalidation Condition: system-defined condition that changes/voids a view.
AI Analysis: DeepSeek language and choice over legal candidates.
System Fact: data produced by ingestion/model/policy.
Provenance: source IDs, versions, hashes, and times.

First Seen Odds != Opening Odds. Reference Score != Exact Score Recommendation. Candidate != Recommendation. AI confidence != model probability. Market probability != independent model probability. AI text != system fact.
