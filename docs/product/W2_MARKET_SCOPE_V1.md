# W2 Market Scope V1

Phase 1 formal markets: 1X2, Asian Handicap, Over/Under.

Phase 2 research market: BTTS. BTTS can only be WATCH/RESEARCH until separately backtested and calibrated.

Explanatory only: Exact Score. It may appear as Reference Score or match scenario but cannot be a Stage 1 primary recommendation.

Unsupported: player props, corners, cards, half/full time, parlays, live, mixing advancement with 90-minute markets, and special markets without clear settlement rules.

All candidates must come from real system markets and lines. DeepSeek cannot create a nonexistent market or line. Knockout matches must separate 90-minute result from advancement.
