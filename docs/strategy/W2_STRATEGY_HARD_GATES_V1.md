# W2 Strategy Hard Gates V1

Hard gates reject or downgrade shadow opportunities when kickoff has passed,
quotes are stale/live/suspended, same-line pairing is invalid, model or
calibration evidence is missing, settlement is unclear, prices are below adjusted
minimum odds, or risk-adjusted EV is non-positive.

Stable reason codes are defined in `config/policies/strategy_hard_gate.v1.json`.
